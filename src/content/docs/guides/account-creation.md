title: Create Account
description: Create and verify your Notificator account from the mobile app.
---

## Prerequisites

- Mobile app installed.
- Internet connection.

## Account creation steps

1. Open the mobile app.
2. Choose Sign Up.
3. Enter your email and password.
4. Complete verification flow if prompted.
5. Sign in and open the Settings/Profile tab.

## Recommended first checks

- Confirm your profile email is correct.
- Confirm app session persists after restart.
- Confirm you can open API Keys section.

## Next step

Create your integration key in [Create API Key (Mobile)](/guides/mobile-api-key-creation/).
